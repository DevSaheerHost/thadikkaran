Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Thadikkaran Barbershop ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Thadikkaran appointment-booking app and website (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you book an appointment, we collect:

  • Name and email address — obtained through Google Sign-In when you log in.
  • Phone number — which you provide so we can contact you about your booking.
  • Booking details — the service you choose, date, time, and appointment status.
  • Device notification token — if you allow notifications, so we can send you booking reminders.

We do not collect payment details. Payment is made in person at the shop.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, manage, and confirm your appointments.
  • To contact you about your booking if needed.
  • To send appointment reminders and notifications (only if you allow them).
  • To improve our service and prevent misuse such as repeated no-shows.

3. How Your Information Is Stored

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database, and Cloud Messaging) and the website is hosted on Vercel. These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy standards.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data with the trusted service providers listed above (Google Firebase and Vercel) strictly to operate the Service.

5. Data Retention

We keep your booking information for as long as needed to run the salon and maintain booking history. You may request that we delete your personal data at any time by contacting us (see below).

6. Your Rights

  • You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • You can request correction or deletion of your data.
  • You can revoke notification permission anytime from your browser or phone settings.
  • You can sign out at any time to stop using the Service.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for general use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
